LIGHT ROCKET LAUNCHERS

A rocket is a self-propelled ammunition without guidance, i.e. a projectile that provides the energy for its propulsion and moves in a straight line. They differ from conventional bullets, which are propelled by a single explosion, and missiles, which are guided over part or all of their course. Unlike bullets, rockets accelerate after they are fired. The following rocket launchers give priority to precision and range over impact power. They can be considered as advanced precision weapons, which are obviously not accessible to civilians.
