MISSILES LAUNCHERS

A missile as defined here is a self-propelled and guided munition, usually by an internal target acquisition system and more rarely remotely. These portable high-tech weapons appeared in the 2000s, when the miniaturization of the target detection and piloting organs became really appreciable.
Modern portable missiles are naturally intended for use in armored vehicles, but lighter versions are used as anti-personnel or anti-cyborg weapons.
