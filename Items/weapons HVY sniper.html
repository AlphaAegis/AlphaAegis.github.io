HEAVY SNIPER RIFLES

If you're a bear, a modern Lucky Luke, a tank that no classic bullet can stops, that's what they'll use against you. The C-Swat, the armed forces, the corporatist militias are all well equipped with these weapons for typical military use (don't look for them in your favorite armory, they are illegal). Killing your target on first try, no matter how well protected, or stopping a vehicle a kilometer away is what one can expect from this type of toy.
