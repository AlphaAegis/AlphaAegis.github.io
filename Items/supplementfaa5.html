Review

As its name suggests, it is a supplement on Europe, the "Plus" indicating a revised version. It contains a description of European political and military structures, plus a country-by-country description, covering an area roughly from Portugal to Romania. In addition, there is a description of the different roles (solo, netrunner, media...) with the particularities of Europe. For example, a solo should play it more subtle than in the Combat Zone ! Finally some technical "gadgets", such as biotech. It's still expensive and not super performant, but already promising.
