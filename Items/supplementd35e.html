Review

Predecessor of Deep Space, this supplement describes the orbit, and is very focused on hardware and rules, rather than the background of the region. It can easily be considered as a first draft before Deep Space that will deepen what is missing in this supplement. Since most of this supplement is included in its successor, do not hesitate to do without Near Orbit.
