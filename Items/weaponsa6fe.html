DART GUNS

In 2020, dart weapons are no longer toys for kids. Even if their damage is limited, they can prove to be good riot weapons. With the help of soporific products or neurotoxins, they can prove to be far more dangerous than one might think at first glance.
A low-powered needle gun coated with a military virus culture can be a deadlier weapon than a 12 millimeter. It's all a matter of patience.
The conquest of space has breathed new life into these weapons : in an environment where a bullet impact or even a spark can have catastrophic repercussions, the use of safety needle weapons, breaking against hard surfaces, is recommended.
