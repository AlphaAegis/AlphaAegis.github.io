Harley Roadhome
Type : Motorcycle
Top speed : 160 km/h
Acc/dcc : 15/40
Crew : 1
Passengers : 3
Range : 440 km 		Cargo : 800 kg
Maneuver : -2
SDP : 65 (Body 3)
SP : 30 (Armor 2)
Mass : 1.275 ton
Cost : 59 500 eb
Reference : Datafortress 2020
As of yet, Harley is the only company to design vehicles specifically for its nomad clientele. Realizing the cultural impact they have had on the nomads, and the fact that nomads are Harley's number one customers, Harley has now produced the Roadhome.
The Roadhojme is basically a small shelter built onto a large trike. It isn't really designed to be slept in, but its seats recline flat so that in bad weather conditions you can sleep inside it. What it really does is provide the feeling of home for nomads on solitary long hauls and allows them to carry a little more than they would be able to on a regular bike. It also provides protection from the elements without the claustrophobia of standard enclosed bikes. It has its own water supply, complete with a retractable outside hose for taking showers, built in water purifier, small microwave and stove, and a large storage compartment. It is available in 2 or 4 man versions.

Special Equipment :
Limited off-road capability, audio entertainment suite, GPS, 40 litres water supply and pump, water filtration unit, microwave, stove, heat/air conditioning, seondary battery.
