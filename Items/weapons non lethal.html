NON LETHAL WEAPONS

These weapons are freely available for sale because they do not pose a direct danger to those against whom they are directed. They include remote tasers, which are improved versions of current electric-type defensive weapons, disabling weapons, which immobilize or impede target movement, and impact weapons, which have the effect of a violent blow.
