FASHION SHOPPING AT THE NAKAJIMO COMPLEX

Located in Beautiful Downtown Night City, the Nakajimo Complex is home to some of the finest clothiers in the area. These clothiers are an especially priveledged lot because the owners of the Complex have arranged for exclusive west coast rights to some of Asia's hottest designers. Because these designers are so accomplished across the entire board of fashion and design, their wares are divided amongst the stores that have paid for the priviledge of carrying them.
