WONDERNINES

You may find that 9 mm guns are somewhat weak in the Cyberpunk world. However, in the civilian self-defence market, they are considered the most powerful calibre weapons available. 9 mm pistols are easy to use, usually precise, they come with large capacity clips and have affordable prices in the plastic polymer versions. All these features make them the most common handguns found on the streets.
