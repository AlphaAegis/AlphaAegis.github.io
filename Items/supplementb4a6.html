Review

This scenario is a continuation of Necrology I, to form a plot in continuity, by re-staging actors from the previous scenario. The voodoo theme is original, and skillfully mixes beliefs and technologies to confuse players accustomed to a supernatural universe, or on the contrary, overly rational players. This scenario, which is openly inspired by Gibson's novels, is an excellent sequel to the first volume. Note that it has a little more action. Alternate Reality supplements such as Dark Metropolis, Night's Edge and Grimm's Cybertales are recommended.
