In this scenario, evil corpos make a life-size test of a bacteriological weapon that turns any random person into a psychopathic killer. As you can imagine, this scenario is going to be a fine one. Small originality, it takes place in the ruins of Las Vegas, and so an embryo of background is provided for the city.
