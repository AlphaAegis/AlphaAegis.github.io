Review

The first of the (sad) series published by Atlas Games. The PCs are in a hostel and are hired for a risky job where they will earn XP points. The never-ending story, not interesting for two bucks. At least Atlas Games will have remained true to itself throughout their publication : it's bad from beginning to end.
