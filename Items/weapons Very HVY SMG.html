VERY HEAVY SUBMACHINE GUNS

Heavy SMGs have flourished after the year 2000, mainly due to the appearance of light personal armour. They are now bulkier and so less concealable, with a firepower that requires a strong hand to be hold them. Usualy the equivalent of the previous century's rifles in every aspect but the size. Heavy SMGs are primarly used by police corps, military commandos, and above all corporate forces or militias.
