BORG AND ACPA HANDGUNS

In 2020, extraordinary fighters must be provided with extraordinary weapons. The widespread use of cybernetic implants in the armed forces, militias and police has opened a market for weapons that no normal man could handle safely. Thus, the weapons described below require at least a cyberarm. Some can only be used by a combatant moving around in ACPA. Most of these weapons are rare and should only appear in a game to scare your players.
