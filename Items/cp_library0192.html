Mirrorshades: The Cyberpunk Anthology


Mirrorshades was the first anthology of cyberpunk authors. The writers in this collection can be considered the first wave of cyberpunk science fiction. Since those halcyon days, many more authors have tripped the silicon floorboards. Like jazz or reggae, cyberpunk has grown beyond itself yet contains its root brainstem.


Contents

  • Preface by Bruce Sterling.

  • The Gernsback Continuum by William Gibson. Also contained in Burning Chrome.

  • Snake-Eyes by Tom Maddox.

  • Rock On by Pat Cadigan.

  • Tales of Houdini by Rudy Rucker.

  • 400 Boys by Marc Laidlaw.

  • Solstice by James Patrick Kelly.

  • Petra by Greg Bear.

  • Till Human Voices Wake Us by Lewis Shiner.

  • Freezone by John Shirley.

  • Stone Lives by Paul Di Filippo.

  • Red Star, Winter Orbit by Bruce Sterling and William Gibson.

  • Mozart in Mirrorshades by Bruce Sterling and Lewis Shiner.
