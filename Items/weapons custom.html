CUSTOM-MADE WEAPONS

Custom-made weapons are extremely expensive and rare gems. They are only available on order if you know a very talented gunsmith who is willing to make the weapon for you. This type of weapon is reserved for professionals who are not too eager to spend money and who want weapons of excellent quality and very good precision. They are of course highly illegal because they are intended to kill for certain.

Cyberdyn offers a complete range of war weapons for special customers (elite units, secret government agencies, etc). These exceptional weapons are available only on order, hence the impossibility to find them except perhaps on a corpse...

This weapon, made only on special order by Cyberdyn, is the pinnacle in the art of precision shooting. It is capable of destroying any target, tank, assisted combat armor, infantryman behind heavy cover, with unparalleled accuracy. Closer to a cannon than a rifle, it can only be handled on its bipod, even if the shooter is a borg, given its high bulk. It is equipped with an oversized 36 cm long magazine as well as an impressive sighting system :
- Aiming computer (+2 at medium range, +3 at long and extreme range) including low light vision and heat detection (infrared).
- Cyber-optical triangulation system (requires a smartgun link) : allows you to visualize where the weapon is pointing and to zoom in for long-distance shooting. The accuracy bonus is +3 at all distances and the malus due for moving targets is reduced by 2.
It is necessary to change the battery after each empty charger.
