BOWS AND CROSSBOWS

Don't laugh, string weapons are commonplace in 2020 for several reasons :
- Their legality : these weapons are freely available and can be carried freely. However, they are attracting a lot of attention...
- Their low price : for 150 eb, you can have a very deadly weapon.
- Their discretion : no flame, no smoke or detonation, an appreciable accuracy at 100 meters and the possibility to dismantle some bows in two parts make them good sniper weapons.
- Their damage : the largest bows have an impact equivalent to that of a large-caliber rifle. In addition, modified arrows (see ammunition chapter) allow to increase this damage even more.
Bows are mostly found among some atypical solos, nomads and independent Indian tribes.
