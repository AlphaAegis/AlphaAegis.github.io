HEAVY SUBMACHINE GUNS

Unlike handguns that fire in single shot mode, 10 mm weapons like those described in this page can be considered as heavy. I mean, that a SMG is heavy when a normal human being can't use it with a single hand correctly. Even if some 10 mm SMGs are easy to conceal, their fire power can be classified as high. Legally, like any other SMG, those weapons are forbidden in the 2020 city streets.
