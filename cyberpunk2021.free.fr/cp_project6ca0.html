Zippies

The Cyberpunk Project

 

Love, peace, and cyberspace...


Zippies, or cyber-hippies, are a group somewhere between cyberpunks and hippies. Ideology includes psychedelia and techno-optimism.

Timothy Leary is the most well-known zippy-guru.
