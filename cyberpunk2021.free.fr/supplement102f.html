Review

In this scenario, a rich eccentric tries to get his hands on a werewolf specimen, and to do so, resorts to violence. Vampires, werewolves, shamanic magic... this is a scenario that is not lacking in exoticism, where players can play basic punks, mercenaries, werewolves, vampires, American Indians, etc. Since this scenario is mainly horror and fantasy oriented, it is recommended to have at least the Night's Edge booklet to play it.
