Review

On the back of the supplement it says "you're bad". For my part I would say "that's bad". Indeed this supplement gathers 250 weapons contained in the Cyberpunk supplements. To top it all off, they are not illustrated, only a few decorate the middle of the supplement. So here is a booklet that will not have cost much to Talsorian : old illustrations, copy-paste text and voilà, a new supplement. If you absolutely want to have your weapons catalog, you can give in... it's your money, not mine.
