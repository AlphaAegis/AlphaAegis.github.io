Review

Despite a somewhat ridiculous and lurid subtitle "Multimedia in Cyberpunk age", this supplement, worthy successor of Rockerboy, proposes to not limit itself to show-business, but to all media, from pirate radios to TV majors and braindance, their power and counter-powers from the underground. I prefer to reserve my judgment until I reread the supplement, but this one seems to me to be of great quality, despite the very ugly illustrations.
