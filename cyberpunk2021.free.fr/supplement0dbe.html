Review

This scenario tackles the theme of vampires head-on without prevarication. It's a plot that mixes hi-tech and vampirism, tribal gangs and corporations. It's quite a mix, which can break the routine of a team a little too used to a "normal" world. Alternate Reality supplements such as Night's Edge in particular are therefore recommended.
