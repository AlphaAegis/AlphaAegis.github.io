Review

This is a new scenario that takes the PCs to the end of the night with voodoo, vampires, and a plot that flirts with the supernatural. Connoisseurs will be delighted, provided they get out of the Cyberpunk frame. Alternate Reality supplements such as Dark Metropolis, Night's Edge and Grimm's Cybertales are recommended for this scenario.
