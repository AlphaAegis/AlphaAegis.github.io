Review

This supplement plunges us in 2202 into the baroque world of Alec Effinger's novel of the same name. Not having finished reading it, I prefer to reserve my judgment but this supplement, offbeat and original, seems to me to be able to provide original inspirations for scenarios featuring the Middle East and Islam. Another supplement is taken from the universe of a novel : Hardwired.
