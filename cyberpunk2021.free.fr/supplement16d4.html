Review

Second part of the campaign, war takes there a considerable scale and prosaically prepares the 3rd edition of Cyberpunk. To be convinced of it, it is enough to note that pages of Maximum Metal are reproduced there. No more playing the poets, you are warned. When Militech and Arasaka come to their hands, and you are in the middle... Always the same format for the scenarios, more equipment and weapons, the presence of legends of Cyberpunk, a flawless background and a few surprises contribute to make this campaign very attractive although very deadly.
