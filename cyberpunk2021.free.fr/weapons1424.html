SMALL HANDGUNS

Three types of user carry this kind of gun in 2020 :
- Featherweights, who are afraid of twisting their wrist while shooting with a 9 mm.
- Embittered, who think that in any case they won't make up the weight, and that a light 5 millimeters won't encumber them too much, while they run to the nearest shelter.
- The professionals, who always aim (and hit) the head. And nobody needs a hand-cannon to thrash a solo if this one is hurt in the head. Some people think it's an advantage to look like an amateur, by showing off a .38 gun.
In Europe, in Japan or in third world countries, where Kevlar suit is not yet in fashion or too expensive, such weapons are very more common.
