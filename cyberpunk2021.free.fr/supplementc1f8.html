Review

A new volume about corporations, this time focusing on two giant companies in the oil, mining and food industry : Petrochem and SovOil. Nothing more to say than in the two previous volumes. We will appreciate here the fact that they are corporations focused on more traditional fields (energy, food, etc) and therefore much closer to the general public than Arasaka or Militech. One will also regret the manichean aspect of the American vs. Russian corpo... Concerning the Russian corporation, all the clichés are there, and the background is not very original. It's a pity.
