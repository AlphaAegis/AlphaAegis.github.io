ACPA

Although ACPA (Assisted Combat Powered Armor) is a relatively new technology, several companies are already producing suits for military and corporate use. Presented here are some PAs from major manufacturers that are now hitting the market. These listings reflect the basic suits as they come from the factory, and, as with any expensive and prestigious vehicle, there are always numerous options which may be added (for an additional charge, of course).
