Review

This supplement analyses a Night City club founded by veterans of the Central American War. The NPCs and the club are quite detailed, but we would have liked more details on the "community" aspect of the veterans. Presented as an interview, the first 25 pages are dedicated to the club, and the next 80 are a compilation of scenarios, each one more basic than the other, whose only common point is that the NPCs are recruited from the above-mentioned club. This supplement is nice, but instead of 8 mediocre scenarios, we would have preferred a deepening of the club and of the parallel activities of which it is the hub, the mutual aid network of the "south-am vets".
