Review

This supplement is devoted exclusively to the matrix. It is composed of 3 sections : 30 pages concerning the hardware, 30 the software, then about forty to adapt the Netrunner card game to Cyberpunk 2020. Big disappointment, nothing new comes to brighten up the matrix : more soft, more hardware, but a certain amount of previous supplements... It's just a laconic catalog served by poor quality black and white images. At the extreme rigor, if you're a fan of Netrunner CCG and Cyberpunk, this supplement will have some interest for you. Otherwise, you can abstain without regret.
