Background
Aramaki is the sly chief of Section 9. He is a shrewd player and has been in the intelligence business for as long as anyone can remember. He is the kind of guy who always has something up his sleeve and isn't afraid to go head to head with politicians. Despite his trickiness he does seem to care about what happens to those under his command and will put his own ass on the line to help them out.
Aramaki, as Togusa, is fully human (aside from a cyberbrain).
