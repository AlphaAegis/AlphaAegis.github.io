Review

An Arasaka submarine carrying weapons (logic for a submarine ?!) lost at sea, and the PCs have to take care of it : enough to make an original underwater scenario. Unfortunately, we only have here a brief and rather unrealistic synopsis, a pretext for some action scenes. Given the general quality of the scenarios published by Atlas Games, this one does not contrast with the rest.
