Review

This scenario, for once in the Alternate Reality series, has nothing to do with the supernatural. A sordid investigation into a story of corporate betrayal, cloning and cult influence will plunge players into the heart of the most sinister plots. In addition to its originality, this scenario is made with the usual Ianus Games care. Alternate Reality supplements such as Dark Metropolis, and Grimm's Cybertales are recommended to complete this excellent scenario.
