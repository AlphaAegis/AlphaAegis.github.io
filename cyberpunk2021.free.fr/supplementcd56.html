Review

This supplement could have been good. Unfortunately, the American vision of Europe, apart from being kindly unrealistic, is based on erroneous or inaccurate data that punctuate the pages of this supplement (did you know that the gendarmerie was the French police ?). Some nice inspirations are scattered throughout the pages, but the general feeling after reading is to have a supplement that is a bit empty. Instead, move on to the legitimate successor to this supplement : EuroSource Plus.
