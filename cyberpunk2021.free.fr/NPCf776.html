Background
Yoshi is Hitomi's lover. He is a landmate mechanic who much to Hitomi's aggravation often seems more interested in motorcycles and landmates than anything else. This boy is a machine freak. He spends every thing he earns and is always close to being in debt, perhaps if Deunan ever learns to take care of her LM Yoshi will be broke and bankrupt. Yoshi is fairly close minded but his naivete makes it ok, like Hitomi most everybody he meets likes him.
