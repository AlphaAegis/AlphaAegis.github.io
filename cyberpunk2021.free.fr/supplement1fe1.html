Review

This is a detailed guide to the United Kingdom and England in particular. Deepening Eurosource and EuroSource+, this supplement brings real depth to the Perfidious Albion by detailing daily life, the diet, the media, the different regions of the country, the neo-Pagan and terrorist movements, etc. Very complete, this supplement is simply indispensable if you plan to play outside the US. What a pity that such a supplement was not created for France or Germany.
